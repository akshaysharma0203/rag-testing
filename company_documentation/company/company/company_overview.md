# NexaCore Technologies — Company Overview

## About Us
NexaCore Technologies is a global IT services and software solutions company founded in 2010, headquartered in Austin, Texas, USA. With over 2,400 employees across 18 countries, NexaCore delivers cutting-edge technology solutions to enterprises across finance, healthcare, retail, logistics, and government sectors.

We specialize in cloud infrastructure, AI/ML platforms, enterprise software, cybersecurity, and digital transformation consulting. Our mission is to empower organizations with intelligent, scalable, and secure technology that drives measurable business outcomes.

## Vision
To be the most trusted technology partner for enterprises navigating the digital future.

To deliver innovative, reliable, and human-centered technology solutions that solve real business problems at scale.
