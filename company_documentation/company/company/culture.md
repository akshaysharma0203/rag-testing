# NexaCore Technologies — Culture & Values

## Our Culture

At NexaCore, culture is not a poster on the wall — it is how we hire, how we work, how we lead, and how we grow. We believe that the best technology is built by teams that feel psychologically safe, deeply motivated, and genuinely included.

## How We Work

### Hybrid-First
NexaCore operates on a hybrid-first model. Most employees work 2–3 days per week from the office and the rest remotely. We trust our people to manage their time effectively and deliver results without micromanagement.

### Async by Default
We respect deep work. Non-urgent communication happens asynchronously through Slack and Notion. Meetings are intentional, short, and outcome-driven. Every meeting has an agenda and an owner.

### Radical Transparency
Leadership shares company financials, OKRs, and strategic decisions with all employees quarterly. We believe informed employees make better decisions.

### No-Blame Culture
When things go wrong — and they do — we run blameless post-mortems. We fix processes, not people.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

NexaCore is committed to building a workforce that reflects the diversity of the world we serve.

- **42%** of our engineering workforce identifies as women or non-binary
- **38%** of leadership roles held by underrepresented minorities
- Employee Resource Groups (ERGs): Women in Tech, Pride@NexaCore, Black Tech Network, Veterans Network, Asian Excellence Network
- Annual DEI Report published publicly
- Blind resume screening for all engineering roles
