## Overview
NexaGuard is an enterprise IAM platform providing SSO, MFA, privileged access management, and identity governance for cloud and on-premise applications.

---

## Key Features
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Privileged access management (PAM)
- Identity governance
- Lifecycle management
- Zero trust access
- SCIM provisioning
