## Our Hiring Process

### 1. Application Review (3–5 days)
Submit your resume and a short cover note. Our recruiting team reviews every application within 5 business days.

### 2. Recruiter Screen (30 mins)
A casual conversation about your background, interests, and what you are looking for.

### 3. Technical / Functional Assessment
Depending on the role:
- Engineers: Take-home assignment or live coding (your choice)
- Product: Case study
- Sales: Role play scenario

### 4. Team Interviews (2–3 rounds)
Meet your potential manager, peers, and a cross-functional stakeholder. We assess both skill and culture alignment.

### 5. Offer
We move fast. Offers are typically made within 2 business days of final interviews.

## Early Career Programs

### NexaCore Graduate Program
A 12-month rotational program for recent graduates. Rotate through 3 different teams, get a dedicated mentor, and land a full-time role at the end.

### NexaCore Internship Program
10-week paid summer internships for undergraduate and graduate students. Real projects, real mentors, real impact.

### NexaCore Returnship
A 16-week paid program for professionals returning to the workforce after a career break of 2+ years.
